## Key Discovery This Session

Staff mode and Senior+ mode for the Billing story are nearly identical.
The only difference is the first sentence:

- **Staff mode:** Lead with why you stepped in (director asked, you took
  responsibility)
- **Senior+ mode:** Lead with what you found and fixed (ownership gap,
  process redesign)

Same middle, same results. This is consistent with the pattern from
17LIVE and Symphox.

## Two Problems the Design Review Fixed

1. **Duplication** — multiple engineers working on the same thing without
   knowing it
2. **No design exposure** — engineers took tickets and implemented without
   ever seeing the full picture

The design review solved both. Every engineer had the chance to go through
the full design process, not just implement their ticket.

## Billing Story — Staff Mode (Locked)

My director asked me to step in and manage the billing team. When I
arrived, I noticed nobody owned specific features, everyone was doing the
same thing, and we had no clear process. So I redesigned how we worked.
I introduced design reviews with clear ownership, broke down tasks so
everyone understood the scope upfront. This eliminated duplication and
built shared understanding, and kept the team moving. We shipped on time,
and all four engineers stayed.

**Runtime:** ~45 seconds
**Use for:** "Tell me about a time you stepped in to fix a struggling team"

## Billing Story — Senior+ Mode (Locked)

When I took over the billing project, I noticed that nobody owned specific
features. Everyone was doing the same thing, and we weren't aligned on the
goal. So I redesigned how we worked. I introduced design reviews with
clear ownership, so every engineer had the chance to go through the full
design process — not just implement tickets. We broke down tasks so
everyone understood the scope upfront. This eliminated duplication, built
shared understanding, and kept the team moving. We shipped on time, and
all four engineers stayed.

**Runtime:** ~60 seconds
